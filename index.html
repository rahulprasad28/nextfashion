Overview

Next Fashion Apparels are stable suppliers of fabrics and accessories, fully geared up to supply various products in accordance with the clients' requirements in time. We are fully capable of grasping the market situations and understanding the demand of the customers' orders and are able to transform the orders into satisfactory products in time.

Our target and motto is “ to produce satisfactory products in demand for the clients!”

We have a rich experience of 15 years in knitted garment manufacturing. We select the yarn from finest mills from in & around kolhapur and in presence of our technical person we will knit it as per required parameters. Dyeing and finishing of fabric is done at Islampur. The quality of our fabrics are appreciated by buyers since we our dedicated team of professionals are involved at every stage, right from purchasing yarn to finishing stage.
